How it works: Every full 2-hour session costs 4 open-play passes — 2 people trade in 2 passes each to rent the court. Instead of tracking a running balance, the group uses a rotating queue of the whole roster. For each session, the next two people in line who are actually playing that day cover it, and the line picks up right after them for the next session. Players who play less often naturally get skipped less, so it self-balances over time without anyone doing math.
